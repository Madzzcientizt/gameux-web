How We Work
Our process for every game we analyze
Analyze
We look at how each game's interface works — what design patterns it uses, how it gives feedback, and whether it applies things like Fitts's Law.
Deconstruct
We split each game into its core UX pieces — HUDs, menus, onboarding, controls — and look at each one on its own.
Document
Everything gets written up as a proper design critique backed by HCI principles — cognitive load, Gestalt theory, spatial congruence.
